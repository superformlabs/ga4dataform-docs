Privacy Policy

Introduction​

Welcome to Superform Labs (GA4Dataform). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal data is handled in a safe and responsible manner. This Privacy Policy outlines why and how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information. This Privacy Policy applies to all our processing activities, even if we process personal data together with other parties (e.g., during events).

Who We Are​

We are Superform Labs, an organization dedicated to creating smart software solutions for Dataform, such as GA4Dataform. We process personal data of our users to help provide the best possible service and to achieve our objectives.

Data Controller​

The data controller responsible for your personal data is:

Superform Labs (GA4Dataform)
Address: Ahtri 12, 15551, Tallinn, Estonia
Email: support@ga4dataform.com

Contact Information​

If you have any questions, you can contact us via support@ga4dataform.com.

Personal Data We Process​

We only collect and process the following personal data:

  • Email Address: Collected for the purpose of sending you information related to our product launches. Every newsletter contains an unsubscribe option.

Why We Process Personal Data​

We process your personal data for the following purposes:

  • Product Launch Updates: To send you emails about our upcoming product launches.

Legal Basis for Processing​

We process your personal data based on the following legal grounds:

  • Consent: By providing your email address, you give us explicit consent to send you emails about product-related events such as our product launches.

Data Sharing​

We do not share your email addresses with any third parties, except in the following circumstances:

  • Legal Obligations: When required to comply with legal obligations.

Data Storage and International Transfers​

Your data is stored and processed within the European Economic Area (EEA). Specifically:

  • Our website is hosted by TransIP in the Netherlands.
  • The website is designed using a service called Carrd, which may involve additional data processing locations.

We ensure that any third parties with whom we share your data are compliant with GDPR and have appropriate safeguards in place.

Data Security​

We have implemented appropriate security measures to prevent your email addresses from being accidentally lost, used, or accessed in an unauthorized way. Access to your email addresses is limited to those employees, agents, contractors, and other third parties who have a business need to know.

Data Retention​

We will retain your email addresses for as long as necessary to send you information about our product launches. You can request the deletion of your email address at any time by contacting us at support@ga4dataform.com.

Your Rights​

As a data subject, you have the following rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). If you want to exercise one of these rights, please contact us via support@ga4dataform.com:

  • Access: Request access to the personal data we process about you.
  • Rectification: Request correction of the personal data we have about you if it is incorrect.
  • Erasure: Request the deletion of your personal data.
  • Restriction: Request restriction of the processing if you believe we are processing your personal data unlawfully.
  • Objection: Object to the processing of your personal data. We will stop processing unless we have compelling legitimate grounds not to.
  • Portability: Request that your personal data be transferred to another party.
  • Withdrawal of Consent: Withdraw your consent for certain processing of personal data, such as receiving newsletters.

Complaints​

If you believe that we do not act in accordance with the GDPR, we would like to hear from you. You can also file a complaint with the Dutch Supervisory Authority (Autoriteit Persoonsgegevens).

Processors and Third-Party Recipients​

We engage service providers for the performance of our services. These may include third-party recipients such as tax authorities and processors who handle personal data on our behalf, such as hosting services and email marketing platforms.

Limited Use Policy​

(App’s) use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Links​

Our website may contain links to websites of other organizations. We are not responsible for the way those parties process personal data. The privacy statement of the relevant organization applies to this.

Changes to this Policy​

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
